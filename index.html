An Invitation to celebrate Love, Time, and Memories Every alternate weekend, from April 1962 to 1965, Anand Mathur boarded a train from Jamalpur to Kolkata, bound for a date with Kalpana. Miles melted beneath the wheels, and time stood still in those moments they carved out for each other.

This year, on the 9th of May, we celebrate the 60th anniversary of the day that ended those legendary dates and began a journey of lifetime love. And what better way to honor this milestone than with a celebration that lasts the whole year? You are invited—not just to remember, but to relive, to rejoice, and to share in the love that has carried them through six decades. And yes, we are asking for a gift—the gift of your time. A date with them, just as they had with each other from 1962 to 1965. Please select a date from the calendar below and bring a letter, a note , a memory to celebrate a story that began on the (rail) tracks of time and flew into the next 6 decades … think of something more to end…

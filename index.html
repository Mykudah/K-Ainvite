Just Married
Kalpana and Anand

1965-2025

Every alternate weekend, from April 1962 to 1965, Anand Mathur boarded a train from Jamalpur to Kolkata, bound for a date with Kalpana. Miles melted beneath the wheels, and time stood still in those moments they carved out for each other.

This year, we celebrate the 60th anniversary of the day that ended those legendary dates and began a journey of lifetime love.

You are invited — not just to remember, but to relive, to rejoice, and to share in the love that has carried them through six decades.
And yes, we are asking for a gift—the gift of your time.

Please arrange a date with them, just as they did with each other from 1962 to 1965.

The calendar below is open, and we would love for you to bring a letter, a note, or a memory that celebrates your moments with them—moments that began on the tracks of time and have flown through the past six decades.

Just Married
Kalpana and Anand

1965-2025

Every alternate weekend, from April 1962 to 1965, Anand Mathur boarded a train from Jamalpur to Kolkata, bound for a date with Kalpana. Miles melted beneath the wheels, and time stood still in those moments they carved out for each other.


This year, on the 9th of May, we celebrate the 60th anniversary of the day that ended those legendary dates and began a journey of lifetime love.

You are invited—not just to remember, but to relive, to rejoice, and to share in the love that has carried them through six decades.
And yes, we are asking for a gift—the gift of your time.

A date with them, just as they had with each other from 1962 to 1965. Please select a date from the calendar below and bring a letter, a note, a memory, to celebrate a story that began traversed the tracks of time and flew into the next 6 decades....
